Suggested description:
Daneshyar is a bilingual, open-source educational content engine that turns user-supplied university PDF books into grounded question answering, summaries, quizzes, flashcards, concept maps, and presentation drafts. The public beta combines a static GitHub Pages frontend with a FastAPI and Docker backend deployed on Render, supports Persian and English OCR, and can operate with local extractive fallbacks or optional OpenAI-based generation.
